1. Solve for x:   2x + 5 = 15

✔ Correct Answer: x = 5
1Start with the equation: 2x + 5 = 15
2Subtract 5 from both sides: 2x = 10
3Divide both sides by 2: x = 5

2. Expand:   (x + 3)²

✔ Correct Answer: x² + 6x + 9
1Use the identity (a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²
2a = x, b = 3 → x² + 2·x·3 + 3²
3Simplify: x² + 6x + 9

3. Solve:   3x − 7 = 2x + 4

✔ Correct Answer: x = 11
1Move x terms to the left: 3x − 2x = 4 + 7
2Simplify: x = 11

4. Factor:   x² − 5x + 6

✔ Correct Answer: (x − 2)(x − 3)
1Find two numbers that multiply to +6 and add to −5.
2−2 × −3 = 6 and −2 + (−3) = −5 ✓
3So the factored form is (x − 2)(x − 3).

5. What is the slope of the line   y = 3x − 7?

✔ Correct Answer: 3
1The slope-intercept form is y = mx + b, where m is the slope.
2Comparing y = 3x − 7, the coefficient of x is 3, so slope = 3.

1. What is the area of a circle with radius 3?

✔ Correct Answer: 9π
1Area of a circle = π r²
2r = 3 → Area = π × 3² = 9π

2. What is the sum of interior angles of a triangle?

✔ Correct Answer: 180°
1This is a fundamental theorem of Euclidean geometry.
2For any triangle, the three interior angles always add up to exactly 180°.

3. What is the hypotenuse of a right triangle with legs 3 and 4?

✔ Correct Answer: 5
1Use the Pythagorean theorem: a² + b² = c²
23² + 4² = 9 + 16 = 25
3c = √25 = 5

4. What is the volume of a cube with side length 4?

✔ Correct Answer: 64
1Volume of a cube = side³
2V = 4³ = 4 × 4 × 4 = 64

5. The perimeter of a rectangle is 36. Its width is 6. What is its length?

✔ Correct Answer: 12
1Perimeter = 2(l + w) → 36 = 2(l + 6)
2Divide by 2: l + 6 = 18
3l = 18 − 6 = 12

Calculus

Derivatives, integrals, and limits.

0 / 5 answered

1. What is the derivative of x²?

✔ Correct Answer: 2x
1Use the power rule: d/dx [xⁿ] = n·xⁿ⁻¹
2n = 2 → d/dx [x²] = 2·x²⁻¹ = 2x

2. What is ∫ 3x² dx?

✔ Correct Answer: x³ + C
1Use the power rule for integration: ∫ xⁿ dx = xⁿ⁺¹/(n+1) + C
2∫ 3x² dx = 3 · x³/3 + C = x³ + C

3. What is lim(x→0) sin(x)/x?

✔ Correct Answer: 1
1This is one of the most famous limits in calculus.
2As x → 0, sin(x) ≈ x (small angle approximation).
3Therefore sin(x)/x → x/x = 1.

4. What is the derivative of sin(x)?

✔ Correct Answer: cos(x)
1This is a standard derivative to memorize.
2d/dx [sin(x)] = cos(x)
3Similarly, d/dx [cos(x)] = −sin(x)

5. What is the definite integral ∫₀² x dx?

✔ Correct Answer: 2
1∫ x dx = x²/2 + C
2Evaluate from 0 to 2: [x²/2]₀² = (4/2) − (0/2) = 2 − 0 = 2

Statistics

Data, probability, and distributions.

0 / 5 answered

1. What is the mean of: 4, 8, 6, 5, 7?

✔ Correct Answer: 6
1Mean = Sum of all values ÷ Count of values
2Sum = 4 + 8 + 6 + 5 + 7 = 30
3Mean = 30 ÷ 5 = 6

2. What is the median of: 3, 7, 2, 9, 5?

✔ Correct Answer: 5
1Sort the values in ascending order: 2, 3, 5, 7, 9
2The middle value (3rd out of 5) is 5.

3. A fair die is rolled. What is the probability of getting a 4?

✔ Correct Answer: 1/6
1A fair die has 6 equally likely outcomes: {1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}.
2Only one outcome is a 4, so P(4) = 1/6.

4. What does the standard deviation measure?

✔ Correct Answer: The spread/dispersion of the data
1Standard deviation (σ) tells you how spread out the data values are from the mean.
2A small σ means values are clustered close to the mean; a large σ means they are more spread out.

5. In a normal distribution, approximately what % of data falls within 1 standard deviation of the mean?

✔ Correct Answer: 68%
1This is the 68-95-99.7 rule (Empirical Rule) for normal distributions.
2Within 1σ of the mean → ~68% of data
3Within 2σ → ~95%, and within 3σ → ~99.7%

2List multiples of 6: 6, 12, 18...
3The smallest common multiple is 12.

12⁵ means 2 multiplied by itself 5 times.

✔ Correct Answer: $60
