MathSphere is an interactive mathematics learning website developed as a university project by Tharushi Subhani, a second-year undergraduate student at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka. This platform is designed to help students understand mathematical concepts in a simple and engaging way through explanations, practice questions, and interactive tools.
